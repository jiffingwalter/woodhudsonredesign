Volunteer With Us

Volunteers are the Backbone of Our Organization

Would you like to share your time and talents to help our organization in its mission of cancer research?

Volunteers are always needed and welcome at Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory. Many volunteer opportunities are available, including clerical, answering phones, and volunteering at special events. We have opportunities for every talent and schedule.

Join Our Team!



Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory is conveniently located in Newport, Kentucky, only minutes away from Cincinnati, Ohio.

We recognize it takes dedicated and committed people to continue our mission of cancer research. The team members we hire are trustworthy, dependable, compassionate, and committed to the discovery of new knowledge regarding the causes, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cancer.

We offer our employees a rewarding environment, benefits, as well as opportunities for personal and professional growth. Committed staff working together to support the mission of Wood Hudson is what makes our team environment so successful.

To apply for an open position at Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory, please contact Dr. Julia Carter at 859-581-7249 or email her at jcarter@woodhudson.org.
